Korean Language Summer Camp Applications Now Open!

Registration Open: Join Our Intensive Summer Program

K-CareerNet is now accepting applications for our Korean Language Summer Camp - a comprehensive 4-week immersive experience designed to accelerate your Korean proficiency while providing deep cultural understanding and professional networking opportunities.

Program Details

2025 Summer Camp Session

Program Dates: July 7 - August 1, 2025 (4 weeks) Application Deadline: April 30, 2025 Participant Limit: 16 students maximum Location: Seoul, South Korea with accommodation in Gangnam area

Who Can Apply

Minimum Requirements:

  • Basic Korean knowledge (beginner level acceptable)
  • Age 18 and above
  • Valid passport with minimum 6 months validity
  • Health insurance coverage
  • Commitment to full program participation

Ideal Participants:

  • Professionals preparing for Korean work opportunities
  • University students interested in Korean culture and business
  • Career changers seeking Korean market entry
  • Business owners expanding to Korean markets

What’s Included

Comprehensive Learning Experience

Daily Korean Classes: 4 hours of intensive language instruction Cultural Workshops: Traditional cooking, crafts, and etiquette training Company Visits: Tours of major Korean corporations including Samsung and LG Professional Networking: Meet Korean business leaders and fellow international professionals

Full Support Package

Accommodation: Modern dormitory-style housing in prime Seoul location All Meals: Authentic Korean cuisine breakfast, lunch, and dinner Transportation: Subway passes and organized transport for all activities Materials: Textbooks, workbooks, and digital learning resources

Cultural Immersion Activities

  • Seoul city orientation and historical site tours
  • Traditional Korean cooking classes
  • K-pop and Korean drama analysis sessions
  • Shopping and market exploration
  • Weekend excursions to cultural landmarks

Weekly Program Structure

Week 1: Foundation and Orientation

  • Korean language placement and intensive basics
  • Seoul city tour and cultural orientation
  • Korean business etiquette introduction
  • Welcome dinner and participant networking

Week 2: Communication Development

  • Business Korean conversation practice
  • Korean company visit and networking
  • Traditional cultural workshop participation
  • Korean cooking class and market visit

Week 3: Professional Application

  • Mock job interviews in Korean
  • TOPIK preparation and practice testing
  • Cultural exchange events with Korean professionals
  • Visit to major Korean corporate headquarters

Week 4: Integration and Graduation

  • Final presentations and skill assessments
  • Career consultation and planning sessions
  • Graduation ceremony and certification
  • Alumni network introduction

Application Process

Step 1: Initial Application

Submit completed application form with basic information and motivation statement.

Step 2: Document Submission

Provide required documents including passport copy, health insurance proof, and Korean proficiency evidence.

Step 3: Interview Process

Participate in brief video interview to assess commitment and program fit.

Step 4: Acceptance and Preparation

Receive acceptance notification and pre-program preparation materials.

Investment Information

Program Fee: Contact for current pricing and payment plan options Early Application Discount: 20% reduction for applications submitted by February 28, 2025 Payment Plans: Flexible installment options available Cancellation Policy: Full refund available until May 15, 2025

Fee Includes

  • All accommodation and meals
  • Daily transportation passes
  • Learning materials and resources
  • Cultural activity participation
  • Company visit arrangements
  • Certificate of completion
  • Alumni network access

Success Outcomes

Previous camp participants have reported significant achievements:

  • Average Korean proficiency improvement of 2 TOPIK levels
  • 85% of participants secured Korean job opportunities within 6 months
  • Lifelong professional networks established
  • Enhanced cultural competency for Korean business environments

Application Requirements

Required Documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Passport copy (valid 6+ months)
  • Health insurance documentation
  • Korean proficiency evidence (if applicable)
  • Motivation letter (500 words maximum)
  • Emergency contact information

Health and Safety:

  • Health insurance coverage mandatory
  • Basic health screening required
  • COVID-19 vaccination recommended
  • Emergency medical support available

Limited Availability

With only 16 spots available, early application is strongly recommended. Our previous camps have filled within weeks of opening registration.

Current Status:

  • Applications accepted: Starting immediately
  • Spots remaining: 16 available
  • Priority given to: Complete applications with early submission

Apply Today

Don’t miss this opportunity for intensive Korean language learning combined with authentic cultural immersion.
