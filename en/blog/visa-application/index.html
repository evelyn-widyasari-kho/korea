We’re Now Open for Korean Visa Applications!

Announcement: Visa Services Now Available

K-CareerNet is excited to announce that we are now accepting applications for our Korean work visa support services. Our certified immigration specialists are ready to assist Indonesian professionals with their Korean visa applications.

What We’re Accepting Now

E-7 Work Visa Applications

  • Professional and technical workers
  • Skilled positions in Korean companies
  • Specialized knowledge and experience roles
  • Management and executive positions

Visa Status Changes

  • Student visa to work visa transitions
  • Visitor visa to work visa changes
  • Dependent visa status modifications
  • Visa category upgrades and extensions

New Application Intake

We are currently accepting new clients for:

  • Initial visa consultations
  • Document preparation services
  • Application submission support
  • Legal consultation and guidance

Why Apply Now

Streamlined Process: Our systems are fully operational with dedicated case managers Current Requirements: Up-to-date knowledge of latest immigration policies Fast Track Options: Expedited processing available for urgent cases Comprehensive Support: End-to-end assistance from consultation to approval

Application Requirements

To begin your visa application process, you’ll need:

Basic Requirements

  • Valid Indonesian passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Korean job offer or sponsorship letter
  • Educational qualification documents
  • Professional experience verification

Supporting Documents

  • Criminal background check from Indonesia
  • Health examination results
  • Financial statements
  • Previous employment records

Our Service Commitment

Free Initial Consultation: 30-minute assessment of your visa eligibility Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees with clear cost breakdown Regular Updates: Weekly progress reports on your application status Success-Focused: Dedicated to maximizing your approval chances

Current Processing Times

Standard Applications: 6-8 weeks from submission Priority Processing: 4-5 weeks (additional fees apply) Complex Cases: 8-12 weeks depending on circumstances

How to Get Started

Step 1: Schedule Consultation

Book your free initial consultation to assess your eligibility and discuss your specific situation.

Step 2: Document Review

We’ll provide a personalized checklist of required documents for your case.

Step 3: Application Preparation

Our team will prepare and review all application materials before submission.

Step 4: Submission and Tracking

We handle the submission process and provide regular status updates.

Limited Time Offer

Early Registration Benefit: First 50 applicants receive complimentary document translation services (up to 5 documents)

Corporate Packages: Special rates available for companies sponsoring multiple employees
